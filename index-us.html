О нас

Nuemann Music - we create quality music and promote it. What we do : we create instrumentals, beats in different styles .. mainly rap, rnb, pop recording of voice and intsrumentls mixing/mastering (own material and yours) soc. marketing in facebook, VK, youtube Our team brings together creative people from Latvia, Russia, USA. On each song we work with full dedication to receive a best result. We always have our own sound, non-standard approach. For us, there are no bad artists ... bad material - we will make quality and need sound. And will help in promotion of your music. For all questions about recording, mixing, marketing .. you can write us by email. Creative people: artist, beatmakers - send us your demo.
